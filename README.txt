What the "SE Society" is about:
  - The SE Society is all about connecting the world's software engineering professionals, students, and hobbyists together within an organization that aids in professional growth, career development, internal networking, and knowledge growth within the field of software engineering. The SE Society's website is dedicated to aggregating all information about anything software engineering into an easy-to-use, easily-navigable platform, with such things as local, regional, and global events, conferences, journals, and magazine articles related to software engineering being a few of the many items found on the website.
  
Technologies used:
- Docker, CircleCI, JavaScript, HTML5.
